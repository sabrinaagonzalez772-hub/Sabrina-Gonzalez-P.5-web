Here's some information about me

I really like to ride bikes. Nothing big or extreme, but simple bikes. No dirt bikes or E-bikes. The kind of bikes I like to ride are human-powered. I like these kinds of bikes because I have more control over them. I find bike riding fun and calming, yet somewhat challenging as I must make quick turns and I also must be quick to spot obstacles.

I really like to draw. I enjoy drawing because I can allow my creativity to flow. I find drawing challenging and fun. This is because I need to refine my skills so that I may produce what it is that I have pictured in my mind. But, it is fun because I get to design anything I want, even the most random of things.

I really like to cook and bake. This is because I can try new things and kind of be creative with it. For example when making friend rice I can add oil and rice and then whatever I want next, but it's also fun because the oil splashes and I have to use my brain to come up with a new method to approach the rice. I also like the sound of the oil sizzling, even if it's just oil in the pan it makes the kitchen smell yummy.
